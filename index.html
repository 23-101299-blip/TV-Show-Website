About The Show

When a young boy disappears, his mother, a police chief and his friends must confront terrifying supernatural forces in order to get him back. Set in 1980s Indiana, the series combines childhood wonder and horror as the town faces threats from an alternate dimension known as the Upside Down.

Genre: Sci-Fi, Horror, Drama
Release Year: 2016
Seasons: 4 (Season 5 Coming Soon)
Created By: The Duffer Brothers
